My Favorite Hobbies

  1. Learn foreign languages

    Foreign language learning can be defined as learning of a foreign language outside of the environment where it is commonly spoken. The many cognitive benefits of learning languages are undeniable. People who speak more than one language have improved memory, problem-solving and critical-thinking skills, enhanced concentration, ability to multitask, and better listening skills.


  2. Watch movies and series

    A movie may run about two to three hours, but a series does not have any time schedule. Another difference that can be seen between TV series and movie is in the commercial breaks. We can come across advertisements in-between a TV series, but series hosted on streaming services usually don't have commercial breaks.


  3. Do cardio workout activities (aerobic fitness dance, HIIT, biking, etc.)

    Aerobic fitness dance improves the body through rhythmic and repeated dance steps. High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) is a type of interval training exercise that incorporates several rounds that alternate between several minutes of high intensity movements. Biking is a top-notch cardio workout, you'll burn about 400 calories an hour.


  4. Play songs in the Ukulele and the Acoustic Guitar and sing along

    The ukulele also called uke, is a member of the lute family of instruments of Portuguese origin and popularized in Hawaii. An acoustic guitar does not require electrical amplification, having a hollow body that amplifies the vibrations of the strings. The most obvious difference between the acoustic guitar and ukulele is their size. The ukulele often resembles a small classical guitar, with a much smaller body and only four nylon strings. The ukulele is tuned differently than a guitar with fewer notes and a much smaller sonic range.


  5. Edit photos and videos

    Photo editing refers to modifying or improving digital or traditional photographic images using different techniques, tools or software. Photos produced by scanners, digital cameras or other image-capturing devices may be good, but not perfect. Video editing is the process of editing segments of motion video production footage, special effects and sound recordings in the post-production process, and all of that is done through a video editing software.
