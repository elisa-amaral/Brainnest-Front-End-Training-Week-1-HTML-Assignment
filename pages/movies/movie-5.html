Production Data

Main Characters Description

  • Vanellope von Schweetz is the main female protagonist in the movie Wreck-It Ralph. She is 9 years old and is an official Disney Princess. She was the originally princess of the Sugar Rush game but when she said she didn't want to be a princess, she called herself the president.

  • Wreck-It-Ralph a gigantic but soft-hearted man who is the villain of the fictional arcade game Fix-It Felix Jr.

  • Shank is a character in Ralph Breaks the Internet and the antagonist of the game, Slaughter Race.
  • KnowsMore is the curator of a search engine of the same name. He primarily assists users with finding things in the Net.
  • Yesss is an algorithm responsible for finding the most trending thing on the net, and curator of Buzzztube.

Book Adaptation: Wreck-It-Ralph 2 Little Golden Book by Nancy Parent

Ralph Breaks the Internet is the sequel of Wreck-It Ralph (2012). Wreck-It Ralph 2 Little Golden Book is the adaptation of Ralph Breaks the Internet that was adapted by Nancy Parent. This time, Wreck-It Ralph leaves the arcade behind, venturing into the expansive universe of the internet--which may or may not survive Ralph's not-so-light touch. The book is perfect for children ages 2 to 5, as well as Disney Little Golden Book collectors of all ages!
