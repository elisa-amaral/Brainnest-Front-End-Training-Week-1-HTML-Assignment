IMDb: The Internet Movie Database

The Internet Movie Database (IMDb) is an online database containing information and statistics about movies, TV shows and video games as well as actors, directors and other film industry professionals. IMDb is a searchable database that includes millions of movies, TV and entertainment programs and cast and crew members.

IMDb can help you: Jog your memory about a movie, show, or person on the tip of your tongue. Find the best movie or show to watch next. Registering on IMDb opens up the ability to enjoy the following features: Get personalized recommendations and discover new movies and shows you'll love. Create your Watchlist to track everything you want to watch and get notified when movies open in theaters.

5 Signs You're a Movie Enthusiast

  1. You can talk about movies for hours
  2. You need to watch at least one movie every week
  3. You're always updating your to-watch list
  4. You don't mind watching the same movie again
  5. You search information about the movie cast during the movie
